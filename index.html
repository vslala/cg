Welcome to GitHub Pages.

It is an online Question and Answer and Discussion Website where people can hold their conversation in the form of posted messages. They can also ask different question and any member of Campus Guru can answer or comment if they want to. Apart from just the plain question and answers it also has a blogging feature where members gets a platform to write and share their blogs with each and every person on the globe. It has many more features which I want you guys to experience yourself by registering to the website. Just click and join Campus Guru.#

Designer Templates

The design of Campus Guru has been crafted with the help of Bootstrap and the dynamic contents of the website is integrated with the use of ReactJS (The 'V' in MVC).

Backend is based on the Codeigniter framework. Fast, easy and reliable framework makes it easy to maintain and customize.

Database used in MySql.

Features of Campus Guru

  1. It is an online forum where threads are created in a form of posted messages.
  2. Disqus comments system is integrated to create a healthy discussion environment which takes care of the authenticity of the users.
  3. Spamming and abuses are taken care of and each and every person could report abuse if the content is not relevant.
  4. CG also comes with wordpress blogging platform integrated into the Campus Guru. It provides a person with the platform to share his/her views and rest is taken care by the Campus Guru.
  5. Speed is the essence of Campus Guru. It makes the surfing smooth as silk. Minimalism ;)
